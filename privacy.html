Privacy Policy — InfantContrast.com

Last updated: September 29, 2025

What this site is

InfantContrast.com provides free, high-contrast visuals for infants, designed for parents and caregivers. No account is required.

Information we collect

Directly: If you email us, we receive your message and address. Automatically: Our host/CDN may log IP, browser, OS, and request details for security and reliability.

Cookies: We currently avoid non-essential cookies. If we add analytics or ads later, limited cookies or similar technologies may be used (see below).

How we use information

  • Operate, secure, and improve the site
  • Respond to messages you send us
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal information.

Analytics and advertising (if enabled later)

If we enable analytics (e.g., Google Analytics or Plausible), we will use privacy-friendly settings and short data retention. If we enable ads (e.g., Google AdSense), they will be configured as child-directed and, where possible, non-personalized. You can control cookies in your browser and, if present, via our consent banner.

Children’s privacy (COPPA)

The site is for adults (parents/caregivers). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, email hello@infantcontrast.com to request deletion.

Your rights

Contact us to access or delete information you’ve shared. California (CPRA/CCPA) and EEA/UK (GDPR) residents may have additional rights; we honor applicable law.

Sharing

We share information with service providers (hosting/CDN, security, analytics/ads if enabled) and as required by law. Providers are obligated to protect data and use it only to deliver services to us.

Data retention & security

Logs are kept only as long as necessary (typically up to 30 days) unless needed to investigate issues. We use reasonable safeguards including HTTPS and reputable hosting/CDN.

International transfers

Data may be processed in the United States or other countries. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards.

Changes & contact

We may update this policy. The latest version will be posted here. Questions or requests: hello@infantcontrast.com.
