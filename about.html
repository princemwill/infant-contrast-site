About InfantContrast.com

Last updated: September 29, 2025

Purpose

InfantContrast.com offers a free, simple set of high-contrast black-and-white visuals that parents and caregivers can use on phones, tablets, or printed cards. The goal is calm, low-friction stimulation time—no logins, no clutter.

Who it's for

Adults—especially parents and caregivers of newborns and young infants—who want quick access to clear, high-contrast patterns without distractions. Every baby is unique; follow your child’s cues for distance, duration, and which visuals they prefer.

How to use

  • On screen: Open the page, go full-screen, and swipe left/right (or use arrow keys).
  • Print: Use black-and-white printing on plain paper or cardstock; borderless if available.
  • Duration: Keep sessions short and calm; take breaks and watch for overstimulation.

Safety guidance

This site does not provide medical advice. Always supervise infants, keep brightness comfortable, and discontinue if your child seems overstimulated. If you have any concerns—especially about photosensitivity—consult your pediatrician.

Privacy & contact

We don’t run accounts or ask for personal information. For details, see our Privacy Policy and Terms. Questions, feedback, or licensing requests? Email hello@infantcontrast.com.
